Admission Eligibility

Eligibility Criteria

To be eligible for admission to HUSNBSS, students must meet the following requirements:

  • Academic Excellence: Scored 80% or above in the previous year's Grade 8 Ministry exam
  • Exceptional Potential: Demonstrate exceptional academic potential and talent through previous academic records
  • Complete Documentation: Submit all required documents including birth certificate, previous school records, and recommendation letters
  • Age Requirement: Must be age-appropriate for Grade 9 (typically 14-15 years old)
  • Good Conduct: Demonstrate good behavior and conduct with recommendation from previous school

Important Note

Meeting the minimum eligibility criteria does not guarantee admission. Due to limited seats, selection will be based on merit and additional assessment where necessary.

Application Process

Our streamlined admission process ensures fairness and transparency. Follow these simple steps to complete your application.

1

Online Registration

Fill in personal information and upload required documents through our secure portal. Create your student account and complete the application form with accurate details.

Estimated time: 20-30 minutes
2

Application Review

Our admissions team reviews applications against eligibility criteria. This includes verification of academic records and documents. You will receive a confirmation email once reviewed.

Processing time: 3-5 working days
3

Shortlisting

Eligible candidates are shortlisted based on academic performance and potential. Additional assessments or interviews may be conducted for final selection.

Only top-performing applicants proceed
4

Admission Decision

Successful applicants receive admission offers via email and the portal. Acceptance must be confirmed within the specified timeframe to secure your place.

Offers valid for 14 days
