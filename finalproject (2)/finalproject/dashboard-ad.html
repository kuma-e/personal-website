  • Any pending fees will be calculated and invoiced
  • The spot will become available for waiting list students

System Rules & Admin Guidelines

Rules and guidelines for HUSNBSS system administration

Admin User Rules

  • Admin users have full access to all system functions
  • Admins can register, edit, and remove teachers and students
  • Admins must verify teacher qualifications before registration
  • Admins must ensure student eligibility (80%+ Grade 8 score)
  • Admins should maintain student-teacher ratio (25:1 maximum)
  • All admin actions are logged for accountability

Teacher Registration Rules

  • All teachers must hold at least a Master's degree in their subject
  • Teachers must provide proof of qualification during registration
  • Maximum teaching load: 24 periods per week
  • Teachers can be assigned to multiple grade levels
  • Teachers removed from system must have their classes reassigned

Student Registration Rules

  • Minimum Grade 8 score: 80% (national exam)
  • Maximum class size: 25 students per section
  • Students must maintain 75% attendance to remain enrolled
  • Students with GPA below 2.0 are placed on academic probation
  • Waiting list students are prioritized by Grade 8 score and application date

Removal Procedures

  • Teachers: 30-day notice period before removal (except for misconduct)
  • Students: Parent/guardian notification required before removal
  • All removals require approval from school principal
  • Removed users' data is archived for 5 years
  • Replacement must be arranged before removing key staff
