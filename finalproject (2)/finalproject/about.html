About Our School

Excellence in education for Ethiopia's brightest minds

Our Mission & Vision

Haramaya University Special Non-Boarding Secondary School (HUSNBSS) was launched in February 2021 with a clear purpose: to provide high-quality education for highly talented students in the region who scored high on the national Grade 8 examination.

Our mission is to nurture Ethiopia's brightest young minds through innovative teaching methods, modern facilities, and a curriculum that synchronizes theory with practical application.

Our Mission

To identify and develop exceptional talent through quality education that combines academic excellence with practical skills, preparing students for university success and leadership roles.

Our Vision

To become Ethiopia's leading secondary school for gifted students, recognized for academic excellence, innovation, and producing future leaders in science, technology, and society.

Our History

2020

Concept Development

Haramaya University initiates planning for a special secondary school to serve talented students in the region.

Feb 2021

School Launch

HUSNBSS officially launched with inaugural class of 120 students selected from top performers in Grade 8 national exams.

2022

Expansion

School expands to include Grades 9-11 with full ICT laboratory implementation and 1:1 computer ratio achieved.

2023

First Graduating Class

First Grade 12 students graduate with 100% university admission rate to top Ethiopian universities.

2024

New Campus Development

Construction begins on new purpose-built campus at Haramaya University main campus.
