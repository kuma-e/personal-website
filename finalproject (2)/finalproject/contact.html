Frequently Asked Questions

How do I apply for admission?

Visit our Admissions page and click "Start Application". You'll need your Grade 8 certificate showing at least 80%.

Is education really free?

Yes. HUSNBSS provides 100% free tuition, free lunch, and all educational materials to enrolled students.

What is the minimum score required?

Students must have scored 80% or above in the national Grade 8 Ministry of Education examination.

When does the school year start?

The academic year typically begins in September. Application deadlines are announced in July–August each year.

How can parents contact teachers?

Parents can contact teachers through the school's main office or via the student portal after enrolment.

Is there an ICT lab for students?

Yes — every enrolled student gets dedicated access to our modern ICT laboratory with a 1:1 computer ratio.
